Welcome to the Bills of Trails game!


How to play
1.Start the path on the -color- pin

2.Use the clues on each pin to correctly guess the next pin, you may hit the reset button if you want to reset your path.

3.Once you're done hit submit, if your correct your time gets recorded and put on the leaderboard. If you are wrong you can try again!

Good Luck and have fun!
